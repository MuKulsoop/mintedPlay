Minty Drift: Cyber Streets

PS5, Xbox Series X, PC Released: July 15, 2023
Official gameplay trailer

Minty Drift: Cyber Streets delivers an electrifying racing experience that perfectly captures the cyberpunk aesthetic. The game's neon-drenched cityscapes are a visual masterpiece, with rain-slicked streets reflecting vibrant holographic advertisements and dynamic lighting that reacts to your vehicle's movements.

The custom light system allows players to modify their vehicle's neon underglow, headlights, and even exhaust trails, creating a personalized light show as you race through the futuristic city.

Key Features:
  • Fully customizable vehicle lighting
  • Dynamic weather affecting light reflections
  • Photo mode with advanced light painting tools
  • Online multiplayer with synchronized light shows
Pros
  • Breathtaking neon visuals
  • Innovative lighting customization
  • Pulse-pounding synthwave soundtrack
Cons
  • Steep learning curve for beginners
  • Some performance issues on base consoles
